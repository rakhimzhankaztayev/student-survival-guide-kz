# Kazakhstani Student Benefits
**A curated list of free tools, discounts, and perks for students in Kazakhstan.**

**Who is this for?**
Any student (School or University) in Kazakhstan. *Some benefits will work for citizens of other countries, especially post-Soviet (i.e. Russia, Belarus).*

**What do U need?**

*   **A University Email (.edu.kz):** Not always required, but it speeds up verification significantly.

## Local Real-Life Benefits (Kazakhstan)
Save money on transport, internet, and entertainment in your city.

### Transport
* I live in Almaty and each month I save about 3040 KZT by having Onay transport card
  
| Service | Benefit | How to get |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Onay** (100% works in Almaty)| **~50% Off Bus and Subway Rides and 2000 KZT subscription** | Get a "Student Onay" card. Rides cost significantly less (~60‚Ç∏ depending on current rates) compared to standard fare. Visit an Onay service center with your student ID to make a card. Download Onay app to get access to 2000 KZT subscription|
| **Air Astana** | **Youth Fares** | Special fares for passengers aged 12-25. Look for "Youth" or "Student" options when booking. |
| **City Pass** | **Student Tariff for Bus Rides** | Check local bus card providers in Astana, Shymkent, Karaganda for similar student discounts. |

### üì± Mobile Carriers (Tariffs)
*Check the official apps (Activ, Tele2, Beeline) for the latest "Student" or "Youth" tabs.*

*   **Activ:** Look for **"IMBA JUNIOR"** or **"IMBA PRO"**. These usually offer unlimited data for social networks (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube) and discounted monthly fees.
*   **Tele2:** Special student tariffs often include unlimited calls within the network and bonus GBs for educational resources.
*   **Beeline:** Look for "Bright" (Jarkyn) or similar youth-oriented plans.
